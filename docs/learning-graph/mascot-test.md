# Mascot Style Guide — Pip the Bookworm

This page shows every Pip admonition style for reference. Use it to
verify that mascot images load, admonitions render with the right
colors, and image padding looks tight. Once the seven pose images are
generated and trimmed, every box below should display Pip floated left
against a colored background.

!!! mascot-neutral "A Note from Pip"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/neutral.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Pip the Bookworm, neutral pose">
    Hi, I'm Pip. This is the **neutral** style — used for general
    sidebars, asides, or introductions that don't need a more specific
    tone.

!!! mascot-welcome "Welcome, readers!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/welcome.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Pip the Bookworm, waving welcome">
    Let's read between the lines! This is the **welcome** style — used
    at the opening of every chapter to greet you and preview what's
    coming.

!!! mascot-thinking "Key Insight"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/thinking.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Pip the Bookworm, thinking">
    This is the **thinking** style — used to highlight a key concept or
    a moment where the ideas in a chapter click into place.

!!! mascot-tip "Pip's Tip"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/tip.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Pip the Bookworm, giving a tip">
    This is the **tip** style — used for helpful hints, shortcuts, or
    pieces of advice that make a task easier.

!!! mascot-warning "Watch Out"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/warning.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Pip the Bookworm, warning">
    This is the **warning** style — used to flag common mistakes,
    misconceptions, or things students often confuse.

!!! mascot-encourage "Keep Going!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/encouraging.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Pip the Bookworm, encouraging">
    This is the **encourage** style — used for content that is genuinely
    difficult, where a reader might feel stuck and need a nudge to keep
    reading.

!!! mascot-celebration "Great Work!"
    <img src="../../img/mascot/celebration.png" class="mascot-admonition-img" alt="Pip the Bookworm, celebrating">
    This is the **celebration** style — used at the end of a chapter or
    after a hard concept lands. The dark background lets pale confetti
    and sparkles in the pose stay visible.
