# Senior Capstone Project

## Summary

This chapter synthesizes four years of ELA learning into a substantial independent project. Readers choose from five capstone pathways — senior research thesis (3,000–5,000 words with oral defense), original literary portfolio, rhetorical analysis project, literary criticism portfolio, or civic engagement portfolio — each designed to demonstrate college and career readiness as defined by the CCSS anchor standards. The chapter covers the capstone project framework, oral defense preparation, public letter writing, and the audience awareness that shapes all high-stakes public communication.
