# Speaking, Listening, and Multimedia Presentation

## Summary

This chapter develops the oral communication skills that the CCSS Speaking and Listening strand requires for college and career readiness. Readers build capacity for academic discussion — structured, collaborative, and evidence-based — alongside active listening. Presentation skills progress from basic oral presentation through multimedia and digital-media-integrated formats, including effective use of visual displays. The chapter also addresses formal English, code-switching, civil disagreement, evaluating a speaker's reasoning, media evaluation across diverse formats, and adapting speech to context and task.
